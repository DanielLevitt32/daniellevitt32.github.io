U.S. Tornado Counts

The U.S. is the tornado capital of the world, due to it's location and sheer size of land. Below I've organized number of tornadoes by state since 1950, using data from NOAA's Storm Events Database. You'll notice a general increase in the number of tornadoes as time goes on. There are many reasons for this, including more weather radars, better radar technology and the movie Twister (no, really).
